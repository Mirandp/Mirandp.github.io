Welcome

Let us capture your precious moments.

Studio 54x is a portrait, event, and commercial photography studio in Ithaca, NY. We offer an array of services, including: wedding, maternity, and graduation photography. Studio 54x has served the Finger Lakes region since 2012, so you can count on us to help you capture those moments in time that you'll cherish forever.
